Stephen Curry

Wardell Stephen "Steph" Curry II was an American professional basketball player for the Golden State Warriors of the National Basketball Association (NBA). Many analysts and players have referred to him as the greatest shooter in NBA history. He revolutionized the game of basketball by inspiring his team to utilize the three-point shot on a regular basis. Curry was a six-time NBA All-Star, a two-time NBA Most Valuable Player (MVP), and won three NBA titles with the Warriors.

Klay Thompson

Klay Alexander Thompson (born February 8, 1990) is an American professional basketball player for the Golden State Warriors of the National Basketball Association (NBA). He is recognized as one of the greatest shooters in NBA history. He is a three-time NBA champion for the Warriors, and he is a five-time NBA All-Star and two-time All-League NBA Third Team selection. He was also named to the NBA's best defensive second team.

Draymond Green

Draymond Jamal Green Sr. (born March 4, 1990) is an American professional basketball player for the Golden State Warriors of the National Basketball Association (NBA). Playing primarily at the power forward position, Green is a three-time NBA champion and three-time NBA All-Star. In 2017, he won the NBA Defensive Player of the Year.
